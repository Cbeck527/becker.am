About Me

I just finished my undergraduate degree at Syracuse University where I studied Information Technology. In the early days of my geekery, I liked building computers, working on servers, and troubleshooting tech issues. In my high school, I was the leader of the Lab Manager program.

Things changed when I got to college.

After working at SIDEARM Sports as a tech support representative, I learned what it was like to work closely with a creative team. I became more interested in graphic design, and felt an itch to create. The summer after my sophomore year I taught myself Python and began hacking away at the Django web framework. At the iSchool's NEXIS Research Lab I put this knowledge to use and built applications that utilized social media data we captured. I'm currently learning about building advanced web apps and mobile apps with Objective-C and Python.

I enjoy working on passionate teams and building awesome stuff. I love to combine technical and creative minds and see what can be built.
